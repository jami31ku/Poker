POKER SAMPLE
  • At The Beginning Cards Are Dealt Alternately, First To "YOU" Then To The "COMPUTER".
  • If You Want To Replace Cards Click Button Number Corresponding To Your Cards From Right And Click "Draw Cards" Button. You Can Change Maximum Of 4 Cards.
  • At You May Raise The Bet From $0 to $200.
  • After You Click "Call" Button You Can See The "COMPUTER's Cards".Then It Will Think For Drawing Cards and Now You Will See The Final Hand. COMPUTER Can Bet from $0 To $200.
  • Finally, The Computer Sees Who Won And Then Adds And Subtracts The Ante, Your Bet And The COMPUTER's Bet From The Respective Winner's And Loser's Pots.
